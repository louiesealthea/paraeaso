About paraEASEo

paraEASEo: Return to a Land Called Paraiso is a community-driven environmental project focused on restoring the forests of Subic, Zambales. Through reforestation, proper waste segregation, and community participation, the project aims to protect biodiversity, restore damaged mountains, and improve the quality of life of the Aeta communities.

Vision

A restored and thriving mountain where Aeta communities enjoy a clean, healthy, and sustainable environment.

Project Goals

  • 🌱 Restore degraded forest areas.
  • 🌿 Plant more native trees.
  • ♻️ Reduce pollution through proper waste segregation.
  • 🌍 Preserve biodiversity.
  • 🤝 Improve the quality of life of Aeta communities.
  • 💚 Inspire environmental responsibility among future generations.
